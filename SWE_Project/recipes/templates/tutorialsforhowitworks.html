How It Works

From sign-up to dinner, SousPaw guides the full cooking flow.

The project is now organized as a Django site, so each step below maps to a real route in the app.

1

Create your account

Users can sign up or sign in through the Django-served authentication pages.

2

Customize your companion

Choose your pet species, set a name, and build a cooking buddy with live previews.

3

Search for recipes

Browse the recipe collection and jump into the detail page for ingredients and steps.
