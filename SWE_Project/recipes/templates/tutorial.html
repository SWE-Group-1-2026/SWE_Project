How to Use SousPaw

Everything you need to get cooking with confidence.

01
📋

Create Your Account

Click Sign Up in the top navigation. Fill in your email and a password. You'll receive a verification email — click the link inside to activate your account.

Tip Check your spam folder if the email doesn't arrive within a minute.
02
🐾

Meet Your Pet

After logging in, visit the Pet tab. Choose your companion's name and appearance. Your pet will cheer you on, set timers, and nudge you when something needs attention.

Tip Your pet's happiness grows as you complete recipes!
03
🌍

Browse Recipes

Head to the Recipes tab to explore dishes from Japan, Mexico, Italy, India, and beyond. Use the search bar to filter by ingredient or cuisine.

Tip Save favourites to your Profile for quick access later.
04
🍳

Start Cooking

Open any recipe and press Start Cooking. SousPaw breaks the recipe into clear, numbered steps. Your pet will alert you when timers end and celebrate each completed step.

Tip Keep your screen nearby — your pet sends live nudges as you cook.
05
⏱️

Use Smart Reminders

Within each step, tap the timer icon to start a countdown. SousPaw will alert you when it's time to stir, flip, or move on — so nothing gets burnt.

Tip Allow browser notifications for the best reminder experience.
06
👤

Manage Your Profile

Visit the Profile tab to view your cooking history, saved recipes, and pet stats. You can also update your email or password from this page.

Tip Your XP and badges are shown here — cook more to level up!

Watch the walkthrough below to see SousPaw in action — from signing up to finishing your first recipe with your pet companion by your side.

Do I need an account to browse recipes?
You can browse the recipe list without an account, but you'll need to log in to start cooking mode, save favourites, and interact with your pet companion.
I didn't receive a verification email. What do I do?
First, check your spam or junk folder. If it's not there, wait a minute and try signing up again with the same email. If the problem persists, contact us through the Profile page.
Can I use SousPaw on my phone?
Yes! SousPaw is fully responsive and works on any modern smartphone browser. We recommend allowing notifications so your pet's reminders reach you while you're at the stove.
How do timers work during a recipe?
Each step that requires waiting has a built-in timer button. Tap it to start the countdown. When time is up, your pet companion will alert you — even if you've navigated to another tab (with notifications enabled).
Can I add my own recipes?
Custom recipe upload is on our roadmap! For now, you can explore our growing library of multi-cultural dishes curated by the SousPaw team.
How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click Forgot password? and enter your email. You'll receive a link to create a new password.
